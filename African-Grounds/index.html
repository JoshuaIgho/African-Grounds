About Africa Ground

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate African explorers and filmmakers, Africa Ground was born from a desire to share the continent's incredible diversity with the world.

What started as a small YouTube channel has grown into a platform with millions of viewers worldwide, all eager to experience Africa's beauty through our lens.
